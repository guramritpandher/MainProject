About Our Platform

PDF Summarizer is an advanced web application that leverages the power of artificial intelligence to help you extract valuable information from your PDF documents. Our platform combines cutting-edge natural language processing with an intuitive user interface to provide you with a seamless experience.

Whether you're a student, researcher, professional, or just someone looking to save time, our application helps you quickly understand the content of lengthy documents without having to read them in their entirety.

Key Features

PDF Summarization

Upload any PDF document and receive an accurate, concise summary of its content within seconds.

Interactive Chatbot

Ask questions about your document and get instant, accurate answers from our AI-powered chatbot.

Document History

Access all your previously uploaded PDFs and their summaries from your personal history page.

Translation Support

Translate your summaries into multiple languages including Hindi, Spanish, French, and more.
